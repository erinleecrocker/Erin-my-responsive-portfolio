About Me

Erin Crocker is a graphic designer working on acquiring her certification in web development through Georgia Tech's Web Development Bootcamp. With recent changes in the world and economy she is hoping to use her newfound skill sets to become a proficient developer and designer. She enjoys drawing and painting in her free time, as well as outdoor activities such as; kayaking, biking, and hiking with her son. With her boundless creativity and love of learning every challenge becomes a chance to grow and become more versatile.


In the future Erin hopes to work as a web developer, which compliments her graphic design experience perfectly. Knowing both worlds, she hopes to add both the technical and artistic elements to create the perfect designs.
